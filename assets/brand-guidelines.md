# OpenContext Brand Guidelines

## Colors

- **Civic Blue**: `#2563EB` - Primary brand color
- **Open Green**: `#10B981` - Success, positive actions
- **Context Purple**: `#8B5CF6` - Accent color

## Typography

- **Headings**: Arial, sans-serif, bold
- **Body**: Arial, sans-serif, regular

## Logo Usage

- Use the full logo (`logo.svg`) for headers and documentation
- Use the icon (`logo-icon.svg`) for favicons and small spaces
- Maintain aspect ratio when resizing
- Do not modify colors or proportions

## Usage Guidelines

- Use Civic Blue for primary actions and links
- Use Open Green for success messages and positive indicators
- Use Context Purple sparingly for accents
- Maintain consistent spacing and alignment
